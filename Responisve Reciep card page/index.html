Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper ‚Äì simple yet delicious!

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 20 mins

Total: 35 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Salt & black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, combine with pancetta, remove from heat.
  5. Stir in egg mixture quickly. Serve hot.
